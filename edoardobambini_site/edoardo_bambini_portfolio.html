Curiosity & Drive

I'm an AI-focused full-stack engineer with hands-on experience designing, developing, and shipping production-ready AI applications end-to-end. I have a strong background in AI-driven systems, RAG pipelines, prompt engineering, and product-oriented software architecture, with a founder mindset and high autonomy in early-stage environments.

I care deeply about clarity and usability, especially in technically complex products. I enjoy turning dense or abstract systems into interfaces that are intuitive, predictable, and trustworthy. To me, good engineering is inseparable from good UX: performance, reliability, and visual structure all directly influence how users perceive and adopt a product.

Rather than treating AI as a black box or a marketing feature, I focus on building systems that are transparent, controllable, and reliable. I'm particularly interested in how complex technologies can be integrated responsibly into products without sacrificing user trust or understanding.

One recurring theme in my work has been dealing with ambiguity. I'm comfortable operating without perfect specifications, asking the right questions early, and iterating toward robust solutions. I value teams where engineers are trusted to think critically, challenge assumptions, and take responsibility for outcomes rather than just tasks.
